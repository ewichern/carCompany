About the company!

Ampere Autos' Mission

Ampere Autos exists to deliver the best selection, support, and service for reliable, environmentally-friendly electric vehicles.

Ampere Autos' History

Ampere Autos has been serving customers for over 5 years -- a long time when taking into account that before 2010 the Toyota Prius was the only widely available hybrid/electric consumer vehicle! We provide the highest quality and most reliable electric and hybrid automobiles available on the market today. Our sales personnel are experts in electric and hybrid technologies and are able to use this knowledge to help each customer find the perfect vehicle for his or her needs.
We're also ready to meet our customers' maintenance and repair needs with skilled mechanics and technicians working in our service department.
